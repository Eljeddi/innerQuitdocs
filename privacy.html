innerQuit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how innerQuit collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the innerQuit mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide smoking cessation tracking and mindfulness tools, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Managed through Google Firebase Authentication. This may include your email address or a unique social login identifier.
  • Personalization Data: Your preferred name.
  • Health & Habit Data: Cigarettes smoked per day, cost per pack, quit date, personal motivations, and progress logs.
  • Mindfulness Journal Entries: Optional diary content entered by you.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed securely by Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.
  • Subscription Status: We use RevenueCat to validate and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives a de-identified user ID and purchase receipt data to verify subscription status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (progress tracking, milestones, savings calculations).
  • To personalize your experience.
  • To securely store your progress and journal entries.
  • To verify subscription status.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore).

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Firebase Security Rules are used to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Database): Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management): RevenueCat Privacy Policy

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use third-party advertising services.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your associated data is permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law.
