innerQuit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the innerQuit mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To help you quit smoking through mindfulness and progress tracking, we collect the following data stored in Google Firebase:

  • Health and Habit Data: Cigarettes smoked per day, cost per pack, and your personal reasons for quitting.
  • Progress Tracking: Quit date, journey start status, daily progress logs, and your mindfulness diary entries.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To calculate your health milestones, financial savings, and provide personalized mindfulness exercises.
  • Personalization: To tailor the app experience based on your preferred name and quit reasons.
    • }
  • Progress Tracking: To maintain your quit-streak and store your mindfulness diary entries securely.

4. Data Storage and Security

Storage: Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore).

Security: We transmit all data over secure HTTPS (SSL/TLS) connections to ensure it is encrypted in transit.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase to provide core app services. For more information, please review the Google Privacy & Terms.

6. Account and Data Deletion

We respect your right to privacy. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-App: Visit the "Profile" tab to initiate deletion.
  • Online: Contact us at oussemaeljeddi@gmail.com.

7. Medical Disclaimer

innerQuit is not a medical device. The App provides motivational and mindfulness tools to support your journey but does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Always consult with a healthcare professional for medical concerns.
